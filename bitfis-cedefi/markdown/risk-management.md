# Risk Management

BitFi prioritizes the security and stability of user assets. Our risk management framework focuses on:\


* **Minimizing Smart Contract Risk:** Through rigorous audits and security best practices.
* **Ensuring Operational Resilience:** Diversifying across multiple exchanges and custodians.
* **Maintaining Liquidity:** Employing dynamic liquidity management strategies.
* **Protecting Against Counterparty Risk:** Implementing stringent due diligence and collateralization procedures.
* **Multi-Party Management:** Utilizing Ceffu's MPC technology and trusted parties for asset management.
