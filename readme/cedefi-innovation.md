# CeDeFi Innovation

BitFi's CeDeFi architecture seamlessly integrates the security of centralized finance (CeFi) with the efficiency of decentralized finance (DeFi):

1. **Centralized Strengths:**
   1. Professional asset custody services provided by Ceffu.
   2. Yield generation powered by proven quantitative strategies operating on Binance.
   3. A comprehensive risk management system.
2. **Decentralized Features:**
   1. Support for Liquidity Staking.
   2. Transparent and traceable fund records.
   3. Opportunities to earn additional yields through other ecosystem integrations.
