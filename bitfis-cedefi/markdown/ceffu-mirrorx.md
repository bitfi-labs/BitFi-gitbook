# Ceffu MirrorX

Ceffu MirrorX provides high-speed, efficient access to Binance's deep liquidity. This off-exchange settlement solution instantly mirrors assets between your Ceffu Prime Wallet and your designated Binance MirrorX sub-account.



## **Key Features:**

* **Instant Asset Mirroring:** Real-time asset mirroring at a 1:1 ratio.
* **Automated Settlements:** Automatic, fee-free daily settlements.
* **Flexible Withdrawals:** Withdraw assets from Binance or Ceffu.
* **Enhanced Security:** Leveraging Ceffu's MPC technology and cold storage.
* **Reduced Counterparty Risk:** Maintaining asset custody while accessing Binance markets.
